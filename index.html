About

Based in Luxembourg, working wherever the story is.

A professional cinematographer and photographer with strong editing skills. My work began with film photography — the discipline of light, patience and a limited frame — and grew into cinematography across short films, documentaries and commercial projects.

I shoot, grade and cut, so a project stays whole from the first setup to the final frame. Available for commissions, staff roles and brand collaborations.
